Executive Summary

The GetDraft mobile application has reached a major milestone: the core product is fully functional on a live backend and database. Athletes and recruiters can create verified accounts, build complete profiles, discover and connect with one another through the swipe-based "Draft" experience, communicate in real time, and share photos and videos through a built-in social feed. The sections below detail the modules delivered to date.

01Accounts & Onboarding

  • Secure sign-up with email verification
    New users register and confirm their account with a 6-digit code sent to their email (with a resend option) before entering the app.
  • Guided onboarding
    A step-by-step flow captures role, plan, location and profile details so every account is ready to use from the first session.

02Profiles

  • Fully editable profile
    Users edit their name, location, bio, sport and profile photo. Athletes additionally set position, level, height, weight, date of birth and gender.
  • Map-based location
    Location can be set with a single tap ("use my current location") or by searching country then city, with a live map preview.

03Discover & Matching — the "Draft" experience

  • Immersive swipe interface
    A full-screen card experience: swipe left/right to browse, up to Draft (show interest), down to Pass, plus double-tap to like. Each card's background adapts to the athlete's sport.
  • Smooth, instant browsing
    Cards and their imagery are pre-loaded so swiping is fast and fluid, with the background changing in step with each card.
  • Mutual matches
    When two users Draft each other, an instant "Game On!" match is created, opening a direct line of communication.

04Draft Board — connections hub

  • Received, Sent, Matches & Messages in one place
    Users see who drafted them and can Accept or Refuse; review and Withdraw drafts they have sent; view confirmed matches; and open their conversations — all from a single tabbed board.
  • Conversation access on every connection
    A message action on each connection opens a private conversation directly.

05Real-time Messaging

  • Private one-to-one chat in real time
    Any two users can message privately; messages are delivered to the other device in under two seconds, with no refresh required.
  • Inbox with search and unread indicators
    A conversations inbox lists chats with message previews and unread counts, and includes a search to start a new conversation with any user.

06Social Feed — Posts & Reels

  • Share photos and videos
    From a dedicated tab, users publish photo Posts and video Reels with captions to a feed that switches between the two formats.
  • Likes and comments
    Viewers like (including double-tap), comment, reply to comments, and like comments — a complete and familiar social interaction layer.

07Platform & Infrastructure

  • Live backend and database
    The application's server and database are deployed and online, so the product is fully operational over the internet.
  • Media hosted within the platform
    Profile and post images and videos are uploaded to and served from the application's own storage.
All modules above are implemented and operating on GetDraft's live backend infrastructure.
