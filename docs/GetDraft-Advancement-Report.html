Executive Summary

GetDraft now covers the complete journey from first install to paid subscription. Beyond the core product — verified accounts, rich profiles, the swipe-based "Draft" experience, real-time messaging and a built-in social feed — the platform now includes identity verification (KYC) for every signup, a parent–athlete guardian verification flow unique to GetDraft, phone and Apple / Google sign-in, and in-app subscriptions with native card payments. The two most sensitive flows — guardian linking and a real subscription purchase — were verified end-to-end on a physical Android device on June 10, 2026, against the live backend and database.

01Accounts & Onboarding

  • Four ways to sign in
    A redesigned welcome screen offers phone number (SMS or WhatsApp code), Apple, Google, or classic email + 6-digit code. Returning phone users are recognised and logged straight back in from the same code — no duplicate accounts.
  • Guided onboarding, plan last
    Role → location → profile → identity verification → a per-role questionnaire that feeds matching → tutorial → plan selection. Every account is complete and verified before it enters the app.

02Identity & Safety

  • Identity verification on every signup (KYC)
    Each new user verifies a government ID and a live selfie (OCR, liveness and face-match) before reaching the app — recruiters and athletes alike are real, verified people.
  • Parent–athlete guardian verification
    A GetDraft signature flow: the athlete shows a secure QR, the parent scans it, answers a relationship questionnaire and records a guided video declaration; an admin reviews and approves each link from a dedicated screen. Verified end-to-end on device, including the parent's "You're linked!" credential view.
  • Public profiles with blocking
    Every user has a public profile view, and anyone can be blocked directly from chat — blocked users disappear from Discover and messaging.

03Subscriptions & Billing

  • Three plans — $0 / $7 / $15
    Basic, Starter and Pro give 10 / 30 / 70 swipes per day. The plan step can be skipped at signup (free Basic); "who drafted you" remains free for everyone.
  • Native in-app payments
    Subscriptions are purchased inside the app through the native Stripe Payment Sheet (saved cards supported), with in-app upgrade, cancel and resume. A real $7 purchase was completed on device in Stripe's test mode. Safeguards guarantee one subscription per user — retries can never double-bill.
  • Swipe packs
    One-off swipe top-ups can be bought the same way when a day's quota runs out.

04Profiles

  • Fully editable profile with media
    Users edit their name, location, bio, sport and photos; athletes additionally set position, level, height, weight, date of birth and gender. Photos and highlight videos can be added from the gallery and removed with a long-press; videos open in a full-screen player.
  • Map-based location
    Location can be set with a single tap ("use my current location") or by searching country then city, with a live map preview.

05Discover & Matching — the "Draft" experience

  • Immersive swipe interface
    A full-screen card experience: swipe left/right to browse, up to Draft (show interest), down to Pass, plus double-tap to like. Each card's background adapts to the athlete's sport.
  • Daily quotas tied to plans
    Swipes are metered per plan (10/30/70 per day) with purchased packs adding bonus swipes on top.
  • Mutual matches
    When two users Draft each other, an instant "Game On!" match is created, opening a direct line of communication.

06Draft Board — connections hub

  • Received, Sent, Matches & Messages in one place
    Users see who drafted them and can Accept or Refuse; review and Withdraw drafts they have sent; view confirmed matches; and open their conversations — all from a single tabbed board.
  • Conversation access on every connection
    A message action on each connection opens a private conversation directly.

07Real-time Messaging

  • Private one-to-one chat in real time
    Any two users can message privately; messages are delivered to the other device in under two seconds, with typing indicators and read receipts, and the connection restores itself when the app returns from the background.
  • Push notifications
    New messages and drafts notify the recipient even with the app closed; tapping the notification opens the right conversation directly.

08Social Feed — Posts & Reels

  • Share photos and videos
    From a dedicated tab, users publish photo Posts and video Reels with captions to a feed that switches between the two formats.
  • Likes and comments
    Viewers like (including double-tap), comment, reply to comments, and like comments — a complete and familiar social interaction layer.

09Platform & Infrastructure

  • Live backend and database
    The application's server and database are deployed and online; the production database carries the full schema including identity, guardian and billing tables.
  • Media hosted within the platform
    Profile and post images, highlight videos and guardian declaration videos are uploaded to and served from the application's own storage.
All modules above are implemented and operating on GetDraft's live backend infrastructure. The guardian verification flow and a real subscription purchase were verified end-to-end on a physical Android device on June 10, 2026.
Try it yourself — with two accounts

To experience the full product — including real-time chat and mutual matching — please create two accounts (ideally one athlete and one recruiter / coach, so they appear to each other in Discover):

  1. Sign up two accounts — by phone number (code via SMS/WhatsApp), Apple, Google, or email. Each completes the quick identity verification step. Best done on two phones so you can watch updates happen live on both.
  2. Draft each other in Discover — swipe up to Draft on each account. When both have drafted, an instant "Game On!" match is created.
  3. Open the chat from the match and send a message — it appears on the other device in under 2 seconds, with typing indicators and read receipts.
  4. Explore the rest: edit your profile and add photos, publish a Post or Reel, try a plan upgrade (test card 4242 4242 4242 4242 in test mode), and manage your Draft Board.

Tip: two devices show the real-time chat and matching best — but you can also sign in to the second account on the same phone. A parent account can be linked to an athlete via the QR guardian flow (Settings → Link a guardian on the athlete side).
